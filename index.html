01 — Profile

The person behind every deal.

My name is Shira, and I've spent my career learning that every "no" is just information I haven't used yet.

I started on a trading floor, reading charts and managing high-pressure client portfolios — which taught me discipline, and how to make decisions when the stakes are real. From there I moved into international business development, leading Israeli delegations and national pavilions across Europe, APAC and LATAM. I've managed the kind of week where the plan completely falls apart — like the night our mission to MWC Barcelona had to become a remote operation, hours before departure — and found a way through anyway.

Why business development and partnerships?

Because underneath every deal, every delegation, every cold outreach — there's a person. Technology doesn't sell itself, and strategy is worthless without someone willing to pick up the phone and build the relationship that makes it real. I'm not drawn to BD because I like closing numbers. I'm drawn to it because I like people — understanding what they actually need, earning their trust, and turning a first conversation into something that lasts.

That's the part nobody teaches you in a course. It's the part I'm good at.
